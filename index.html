2022 Kawasaki Ninja 400 KRT ABS

About my Ninja 400 Motorcycle

I named my Ninja 400 motorcycle as "Budz" because he is my buddy when riding.

I am not new to riding but this is my first time having a sports bike so I decided to get this bike as my entry level bike

Also, I like Kawasaki brand because it's my number one top choice and due to their durability, reliability, performance and easy to handle

Key Specifications of Ninja 400

The basic specs for the Ninja 400 are as follows:

Engine: 399cc, 4-stroke, 2-cyclinder, DOHC, liquid-cooled
Transmission: 6 Speed, return shift
Power: 49 hp - 10500 rpm
Weight: 366 lbs (166kg)
Seat Height: 30.9 inches (785 mm)
Electronic Rider Aids: ABS (Anti-lock Brake System)
